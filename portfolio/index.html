Japan's Deflationary Spiral: CPI changes from 1960 to 2020

This chart shows Japan's annual inflation rate (CPI) from 1960 to 2020. The sudden spike exceeding 20% in 1970s reflects the impact of the 1973 oil shock on Japan's heavily import-dependent economy. After this, it declined sharply and has remained remarkably low since 1990.

Data source: https://www.economicsobservatory.com/

Japan's GDP Growth Trajectory 1960-2020

This chart shows Japan's GDP growth rate from 1960 to 2020, revealing high growth during the post war 1960s-70s economic boom, followed by declining growth rates and a sharp contraction during the 2008 financial crisis, with persistently low growth in recent decades.

Data source: https://www.economicsobservatory.com/
