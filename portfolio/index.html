Weekly Coding Challenges

A portfolio of weekly data visualisation challenges from PP434: Automated Data Visualisation for Policymaking, undertaken during the Master of Public Policy (MPP) at the London School of Economics and Political Science. These projects showcase the application of coding and design principles to translate policy-relevant datasets into clear, compelling visual stories using interactive maps, charts, and graphics.
